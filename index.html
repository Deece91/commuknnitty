Commuknitty

Commuknitty is a network of creatives aiming to unite fashion industry talent to inspire, empower and champion fashion's next generation.

The knitwear world - and fashion industry in general - is notoriously mysterious and difficult to break into. We aim to make it more accessible to those from under- represented groups, low-income backgrounds and parts of the country isolated from major fashion hubs by providing mentoring, resources and a supportive community.
